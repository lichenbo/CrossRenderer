Model: Tad.fbx is used, including 51 bones and 96 keyframes per animated bone
Interpolation algorithm: LERP
Instructions: 
	FPS-like control:
		move forward : w
		move backward: s
		move leftward: a
		move rightward: d
		move upward: left shift
		move downward: left ctrl

Open CrossRenderer.sln, compile and run, or you can directly run the executable AnimationDemo.exe inside x64/Debug/
